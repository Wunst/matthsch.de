## Games

### Voxel game

![In-game screenshot, showing a procedurally generated mountainous landscape](/assets/img/voxel.png)

Experimenting with Minecraft style, voxel/Perlin noise based procedural world generation, in
[Godot](https://godotengine.org). I'm not sure what I'll do with the world generator when I'm done
with it/if I'll make an actual game out of it. Right now it's just a research project on procedural
world generation.

Heavily based on [Hendrik Kniberg's talk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ob3VwY4JyzE) on Minecraft 
world generation, linked on the Minecraft wiki.

[Git repository](https://github.com/Wunst/voxel-game)

Browser builds coming soon, to this very site...
