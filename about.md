Hi all,

I'm a physics student, hobbyist programmer and open source enthusiast, born, raised, and based in
[Kiel, Schleswig-Holstein](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kiel). Sometimes I do coding or web design 
for a living, but right now, I'm happily employed at my uni as a teaching assistant (*Tutor*).

In my free time, I play the accordion, cycle, run long distance and occasionally do creative stuff
like making video games/video game mods and writing.

## This site

I bought this domain and hosting so Google can't read my email (although of course now Hetzner 
could, but at least they have to follow GDPR, and I *pay* them to store my data for me). Having a 
website was a fun side effect. 

This website is powered by [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com), the content is in 
[this Git repo](https://github.com/Wunst/matthsch.de).
